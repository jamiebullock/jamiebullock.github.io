About Me

Hi. I'm Jamie. I live in Birmingham and work remotely as a software developer at Noiiz, a music tech startup that seeks to empower the world to experience joy through music creation. I have a PhD in Music Technology, and over 15 years programming experience. I am fascinated by bringing research and development together to create new technologies that enhance human creativity. My main interest currently is musical applications of artificial intelligence. I have three children, and in my spare time I enjoy writing, strength training, playing guitar, and creating more software! Of course, none of this would be possible without lotsa great coffee. ☕️
